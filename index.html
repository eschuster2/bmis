Hiking Tips

Here are some tips for a safe and enjoyable hike:

  • Start with shorter trails and gradually increase difficulty.
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out.
  • Stay on marked trails to protect the environment.
  • Carry snacks and take regular breaks to stay energized.
  • Tell someone your hiking plans for safety.
